## Macrophage Biology & Neuroimmunology Lab

The Macrophage Biology & Neuroimmunology Lab investigates immune cell heterogeneity across tissues and explores how macrophages and microglia contribute to disease processes.

Our research integrates experimental immunology with computational genomics, including single-cell RNA sequencing, spatial transcriptomics, and systems biology approaches.

### Research focus

- Macrophage heterogeneity across tissues  
- SPP1 macrophages in fibrosis  
- Microglia in neurodegeneration  
- Single-cell and spatial transcriptomics  

### Approach

We combine experimental and computational methods to understand immune cell dynamics and their role in human disease.
