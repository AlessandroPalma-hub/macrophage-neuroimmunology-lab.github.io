## Macrophage Biology & Neuroimmunology Lab

The Macrophage Biology & Neuroimmunology Lab investigates immune cell heterogeneity across tissues and explores how macrophages and microglia contribute to disease processes.

We integrate experimental immunology with computational genomics, including single-cell RNA sequencing and spatial transcriptomics.

---

## Research Areas

### 🔬 Macrophage Heterogeneity
Transcriptional diversity and differentiation trajectories across tissues and disease contexts.

### 🧠 Neuroimmunology
Microglial responses in neurodegeneration and lysosomal storage disorders.

### 🧬 Single-Cell Genomics
Integrative computational analysis of large-scale single-cell datasets.
